Our Guests

Asefa Poyan


Asefa Poyan is the visionary founder of the Kabul-Toronto Cultural Home. With a profound and enduring commitment, she has always worked passionately to reveal and share the multifaceted beauty of Afghan culture with people from all backgrounds.

Lina Hakimi


Lina Hakimi is the event coordinator. She is an active and strong woman and a student of Environmental Scienc at the University of Toronto. Lina is dedicated to helping people from other countries learn about the culture and people of Afghanistan.

Zahara Mohammadi


Zahara Mohammadi is an accomplished HR Manager at RBC Bank and concurrently a dedicated doctoral student at the University of Toronto. In her professional capacity at RBC, she applies her extensive expertise in human resources within one of Canada's leading financial institutions.
